**Date:** 2026-05-26

## Decision Framework

A leech card is a card design signal, not a discipline problem. Repeated failure means the card is poorly anchored, too complex, or tests knowledge that isn't ready. Three paths:

1. **Delete** — card tests constructed or contextless material that will be learned naturally from real conversation
2. **Restructure** — card tests real knowledge but the back is ambiguous or the form is buried; add Hint field for the hard word
3. **Replace** — card's note type is wrong for what it's testing; delete and add a cleaner Production note

## Key Principle

Cards 1774207509479 and 1774207509486 were the clearest cases: constructed sentences with no situational trigger.
